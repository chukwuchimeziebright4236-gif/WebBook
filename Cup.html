World Cup Shock: Paraguay Knocks Out Germany

In a massive upset at the 2026 FIFA World Cup in the United States, Paraguay has sensationally defeated heavy favorites Germany 4–3 in a dramatic penalty shootout at Boston Stadium to advance directly to the Round of 16.

Germany dominated ball possession for most of the regulation and extra time, but a powerful first-half header by Julio Enciso gave the South American side an unexpected edge. Though the German squad fought back intensely to equalize, Paraguay held their nerve perfectly during the high-pressure penalty shootout to secure a historic tournament victory.

Football pundits are already calling this one of the greatest underdog stories in modern World Cup history, completely shaking up the tournament bracket.
