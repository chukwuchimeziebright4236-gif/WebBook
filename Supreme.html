🏛️ Major Supreme Court Rulings

  • Birthright Citizenship Upheld: In a major ruling, the High Court struck down an executive order that sought to end birthright citizenship, permanently reaffirming constitutional protections for children born on U.S. soil. You can read the full constitutional case files here to review the legal opinions.
  • Campaign Spending Limits Dismantled: Federal limits on coordinated campaign spending were thrown out under First Amendment protections, giving political party committees massive spending freedom.
  • Transgender Athlete Laws: State-level restrictions keeping transgender athletes from female sports divisions were upheld for West Virginia and Idaho.
  • Assault Weapons Bans: The Supreme Court officially agreed to review current AR-15 and high-capacity magazine restrictions inside Illinois and Connecticut.
